GuardianTech: Empower Your Network's Defense with Cutting-Edge Monitoring Tools

Network Intrusion Detection System – Scans your network traffic to detect and alert suspicious activities that are occurring within your network.

Network Intrusion Prevention System (NIPS) is used to scan your network traffic, alert, and respond when suspicious activity is detected within your network, blocking it. This can make all the difference in preventing your company from being the next major target within your industry.

System Incident Event Manager (SIEM) – used with sensors such as Security Onion to be integrated within this product. A SIEM will aggregate and match event correlations based on the sensor logs that are sent to it."

Simulating Advanced Persistent Threat actors (APTs) – Our penetration testing team can simulate certain APT groups that your company may fall victim to. This process helps identify weaknesses they might exploit, enabling your company to strengthen these areas

Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) – Monitors endpoint devices and sends an alert when it detects suspicious activity. This helps you isolate endpoints to stop the spread of attacks.

Threat Hunting – Our team will identify targets that are most likely to attack your company and create solutions to prevent or mitigate the risk to your company.

Penetration Testing: Our Penetration Testing team will work with your company to identify weaknesses within your network infrastructure as well as among your employees. Strengthening your employees will help prevent future attacks on both your employees and your infrastructure.
